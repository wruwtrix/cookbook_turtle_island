Notes & What to Bring

  • Plan to bring enough for small portions for 6-8 people. I'll confirm how many are attending in the group text.
  • I'll have plates, silverware, napkins, cups, and some serving utensils.
  • I'll have sparkling and still water available. Bring any other beverage you prefer and we'll likely have one beverage from the book available.
  • Use the group chat early to coordinate on specialty ingredient orders! Items like rose hip powder, sumac, dried elderberries, chaga powder, or mesquite flour can be split if multiple people need them.
  • Expect to make some substitutions due to seasonality and ingredient availability — that's totally fine. Most of these recipes are doable if you either order specialty ingredients ahead or plan to reach out to a farm for game meat (bison, duck, rabbit, venison).
  • Choose one dish (or two if you're excited!).
